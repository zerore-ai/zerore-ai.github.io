Who we are & what we believe 我们是谁，以及我们相信什么

We don't stack features.
We define the standard. 不做功能的堆积，
做定义标准的人。

ZERORE is an AI-native, Gen-Z studio. We believe the products that matter are forged inside real scenarios — and the best teams aren't a collection of divided roles, but a group of people who spark and compound on each other. ZERORE 是一支 AI Native 的 Z 世代团队。我们相信真正有价值的产品，必须在真实场景中被打磨出来；最好的团队，不是每个人只完成分工，而是一群人彼此激发、共同进化。

Values价值观

01

Long-termism长期主义

The slow work compounds.慢的东西才会复利。

We don't chase hype cycles. We stack what actually matters, brick by brick — product, tech, data, workflow, customer understanding — so the value compounds.我们不追逐 hype 周期。把真正重要的东西，一砖一瓦搭起来——从基础做起，把产品、技术、数据、工作流和用户理解，层层做深、做稳、做成长期价值。

02

Standard, not a tool做标准，不做工具

Tools get replaced. Standards don't.工具会被替换，标准不会。

Our axis isn't feature parity or pricing — it's whose vocabulary the industry adopts. FICO for credit. J.D. Power for cars. ZERORE for Agent quality.我们的竞争维度不是功能和价格，而是——谁定义的语言被行业接受。FICO 之于信用、J.D. Power 之于汽车，ZERORE 之于 Agent 质量。

03

AI Native by default默认 AI 原生

AI as the first colleague, not a tool.把 AI 当作第一个同事。

We use AI at every layer of our own workflow — research, writing, engineering, customer conversations. Not as a tool, but as our first teammate.我们把 AI 用到自己工作流的每一层：从研究、写作、工程到客户对话。我们不是把 AI 当工具，而是把它当作团队里第一个同事。

04

Grow sideways允许横向生长

Great products grow out of collaboration.新可能在协作里长出来。

Beyond the main runway, we invite teammates to explore side projects and ideas. We believe the best products often grow out of collaborative experiments.我们鼓励团队在主航道之外探索自己的子项目与新想法，鼓励内部孵化。我们相信最好的产品，往往诞生在协作里长出来的新可能。
